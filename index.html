Goal

Our goal is to develop a kinetic sculpture with smooth motions and lights that responds to ambient environmental stimuli. We strive to create a polished product that possesses not only functionality but aesthetic pleasure and simplicity. The simplicity will be portrayed with a well defined connection between a specific input and output.The sculpture integrates electrical, mechanical, software, and design components together, reflecting the spirit of the class, Principles of Engineering.
